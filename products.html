Products
All our products are made with 100% antibiotic, hormone free, USDA inspected human grade quality meats.  Our meat and poultry are raised by ranchers throughout the United States that take pride in producing healthy animals through natural sustainable agriculture. Many are raised right here in the Pacific Northwest.  All ingredients naturally contain vitamins, minerals, amino acids and enzymes of whole raw foods. There are no preservatives, dyes or artificial ingredients added.

Each of our are products are plain and simple. You maintain complete control over any "extras" or supplements your pet receives. We don't believe that any one pet food product, kibble or raw, can be 100% complete and balanced at every meal, for every pet, on a day after day basis. We believe that dietary balance is achieved over time through a variety of raw, fresh, wholesome foods. We humans don't eat the same highly processed foods day after day and expect good health. Why should we feed our pets this way? Because of this, no one product of our foods is a complete and balanced diet in and of itself. They are meant to be components of a raw diet with balance over time.
