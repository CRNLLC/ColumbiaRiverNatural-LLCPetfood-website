Welcome! It's our goal to offer you a more convenient way to feed your pet a healthy, all natural raw diet as nature intended. 

Nature designed dogs to eat a diet based on raw meat. A dogs system gains the maximum benefits from a variety of meat protein sources and bones in their naturally raw state.  Raw foods contain natural active enzymes, unprocessed, highly digestible amino acids, vitamins and minerals that you don't find in processed kibble or dehydrated pet foods. Feeding your dog as nature intended results in happy, healthy, longer lives, and lower vet bills.  You will also see stronger immune systems, elimination of many allergies, shiny coats, healthy skin, strong hearts, clean teeth, healthy gums, more muscle mass and fewer orthopedic problems. Your dog will thrive on raw foods.

Columbia River Natural Pet Foods provides you with many choices of wholesome raw meats, poultry, and vegetables, that make it easy for you to design a meal for your pets specific needs. These ingredient choices are based on the diet recommendations of Kymythy Schultze, author of "The Ultimate Diet: Natural Nutrition for Dogs and Cats", and Dr. Ian Billinghurst, author of the "B.A.R.F. Diet" (Biologically Appropriate Raw Foods).

Our meats are 100% human-grade and USDA inspected. They are US-sourced from ranchers that take pride in practicing responsible animal husbandry and sustainable farming, many right here in the Pacific Northwest. All our meats are hormone and antibiotic free. They contain no grains or preservatives, and are ground partially frozen at the time of processing to ensure only the freshest quality.


As lifelong pet owners ourselves, all of us here at Columbia River Natural Pet Foods understand the care and concern that you have for your beloved animals. We are committed to helping you improve the health, well-being and happiness of your family companions.


We would like to thank each and every one of our customers for your continued support and trust in our company and products. In our photo album section, you can see many of them at work, play or just relaxing with the family. Enjoy!
